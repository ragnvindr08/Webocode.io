W3Schools

W3Schools is an online educational platform widely recognized for its comprehensive tutorials, references, and interactive coding exercises in web development and related technologies. Founded in 1998 by Jan Egil Refsnes and currently maintained by Refsnes Data in Norway, W3Schools serves as a go-to resource for beginners and professionals alike seeking to learn or refresh their knowledge of web technologies. The platform covers a wide range of topics, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, XML, SQL, Python, PHP, Bootstrap, and many others. Each topic is meticulously organized into tutorials, with clear explanations, code examples, and interactive exercises designed to facilitate learning and understanding. One of the key strengths of W3Schools is its accessibility.

Visit W3Schools

SoloLearn

SoloLearn is a popular online platform that offers interactive coding tutorials and challenges for learning various programming languages and related technologies. Launched in 2014 by Yeva Hyusyan, Davit Kocharyan, and Harutyun Mkrtchyan, SoloLearn has quickly gained traction among beginner and experienced programmers alike. One of the standout features of SoloLearn is its user-friendly interface, which makes learning to code both engaging and accessible.

Visit SoloLearn

Programiz

Programiz is an online platform that provides comprehensive tutorials, examples, and references for learning various programming languages and computer science concepts. Established in 2012 by Ankush Singla and Ritika Singh, Programiz has become a go-to resource for programmers of all skill levels seeking to enhance their coding knowledge and skills. One of the key features of Programiz is its user-friendly interface, which makes navigating through tutorials and examples intuitive and efficient. Programiz's tutorials are structured in a step-by-step format, starting with basic concepts and gradually progressing to more advanced topics.

Visit Programiz

GeeksforGeeks

GeeksforGeeks, founded in 2008 by Sandeep Jain, is one of the largest and most popular platforms for computer science and programming-related content. It offers a vast repository of tutorials, articles, coding challenges, interview preparation resources, and coding practice problems. GeeksforGeeks covers a wide range of topics, including data structures, algorithms, programming languages, system design, competitive programming, and more. Its tutorials are well-structured and comprehensive, providing in-depth explanations and code examples to help learners grasp complex concepts. GeeksforGeeks is widely used by students, professionals, and educators alike as a trusted resource for learning, practicing, and mastering programming skills.

Visit GeeksforGeeks
